About us

“Remember the race is for the skilled and the strong, we cannot hope to succeed if we continue to work with antiquated tools and follow old fashion business methods.” _ Sir M. Visvesvaraya.

These wise words are the ones in which we at “DIGI ARKIVE” resolutely believe in. We strive hard to emulate the same by creating innovative models to streamline the very process of learning. We use the latest technology infrastructures so as to provide a seamless user experience for our customers.

Our product is a one stop solution for the entire engineering community addressing all the problems faced by the students they are ready to face life’s next challenges . Apart from providing resources to ace your engineering exams, our product will also provide you with placement related material and preparation material to crack competitive exams for your higher studies. Thus, we would be your companion helping you achieve your dreams .
